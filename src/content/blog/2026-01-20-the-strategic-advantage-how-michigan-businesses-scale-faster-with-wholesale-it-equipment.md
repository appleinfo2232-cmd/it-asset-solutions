In the professional ITAD (IT Asset Disposition) sector, wholesale refers to enterprise-grade assets—typically off-lease devices from Fortune 500 companies—that have been meticulously restored to like-new condition.

A common misconception is that refurbished hardware is prone to failure. The data suggests otherwise. In fact, Enterprise-Grade refurbished equipment (like the Dell Latitude or HP EliteBook series) is engineered to last significantly longer than brand-new Consumer-Grade devices found at big-box stores.
